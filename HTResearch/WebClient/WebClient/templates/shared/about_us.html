Who We Are

--- I think I will put in a short introduction of Dr. Dalla’s work/achievements here --- This project was envisioned by Dr. Rochelle Dalla, Associate Professor at the University of Nebraska-Lincoln Department of Child, Youth, and Family Studies. It was brought to life by a team of computer science students at the Jeffery S. Raikes School of Computer Science and Business Management.

What We Do

Instead of relying on organizations, workers in the field, and researchers to supply relevant human trafficking information, our software uses web scraping and crawling algorithms to collect it automatically. This makes it easy and extremely fast to retrieve and update our data. We also allow those with “Contributor” accounts to edit, add, and flag information to fill any gaps our algorithms leave.

Why We Do It

Through this technology and with increasingly accessible information online, we can search through all the opportunities the web has to offer so you don’t have to. Connect with other advocates, researchers, and volunteers and find a place to help in the right field and area of the globe for you.
